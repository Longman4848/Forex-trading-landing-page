Frequently Asked Questions

A foreign Exchange(Forex) currency trader, is a person who trades, buys and/or sells currencies on Foreign Exchange. Currency trader include proffessionals employed to trade for a financial firm or group of clients, but they also include amateur traders who trade forex for their financial gain or either as a hobby or to make a living.

The foreign exchange currency market, also known as forex, is the world's largest financial market. More than $5 Trillion are traded on the exchange every day-thats' 25 times the volume of global equities The majority of the world's currency is traded through this huge, highly decentralised marketplace.

The Forex market is a global OTC(Over The Counter) trading platform, and therefore does not have any particular location.
